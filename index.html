2016 Jan 06
48 5

Hello, my name is Debbie Millmann.

I want to share with you my experience in losing weight. My weight have always been around 85 kg. For a teenager that was too much, of course. At school, other children ignored me and I was an outcast. I was feeling so unbearable in my body that once I even made an attempt to poison myself with drugs. Hardly but I graduated school and my mom’s colleague introduced her son to me. I think he was a loser and an outsider as I was … Anyway, we got married but as for me I did from despair as I couldn’t hope that someone will show his interest in me.

When I got pregnant, my weight was reaching a horrible figure 100 kg and I have lived with such a weight for 7 years. I had shortness of breath, swollen legs, joint pain, hypertension …

I was rapidly loosing my health. My mom took me to a therapist and he told me that my biological age was 73 years old in my actual 24.

I started searching the ways to lose weights. But the problem was the overflow of information. Everywhere are super diets promising to make you lose 20 kg for 5 days, all those wonderful belts, teas, laxatives and diuretics. I’ve tried virtually everything: Dukan diet, subliminal messages, hypnosis, coding, metabolic diets, training in a gym … But the kilograms didn’t get out of my body. But I began losing my hair, my nails became fragile and brittle, the complexion was grayish … I was tired and irritated all the time, couldn’t sleep well. NOTHING worked!

My husband was not a super model but he also left me.

I just had to do something urgently and not for myself but for my son. I want to see how he’s growing. If I’m sick, I won’t be able to help him with him school assignments, run and play with him.

So, I came home and immediately ordered this product on the official web-site. In two days, the carrier brought me the parcel. So, I received the product and started using it according to the package leaflet.

As a result, I lost 5 kg during the first week!

My face complexion became fresh, my swollen feet were relieved, and the belly fat reduced. What’s most important, I was confident then that I could manage my weight.

In a month I lost 18 kg without any diets and trainings. I ate everything I used to eat but before meals I took Optimum Garcinia. When I lost 18 kg I felt very thin and light and that was a victory! I didn’t want to stop at what I’ve already achieved and in 3.5 months I lost 30 more kg!
